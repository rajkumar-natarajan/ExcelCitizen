# Privacy Policy

**ExcelCitizen - Canadian Citizenship Test Preparation**

*Last Updated: December 23, 2024*

---

## Introduction

ExcelCitizen ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

---

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

ExcelCitizen stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

| Data Type | Purpose | Storage Location |
|-----------|---------|------------------|
| Test scores | Track your progress | Device only |
| Quiz history | Review past performance | Device only |
| Bookmarked questions | Quick access to saved items | Device only |
| Study streaks | Gamification features | Device only |
| App preferences | Theme, language settings | Device only |
| Achievement progress | Track unlocked badges | Device only |

### Data We Do NOT Collect

ExcelCitizen does **NOT** collect, transmit, or store:

- ❌ Personal identification information (name, email, address)
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Contact information
- ❌ Photos or media
- ❌ Device identifiers
- ❌ Usage analytics sent to servers
- ❌ Advertising identifiers
- ❌ Financial information

---

## How We Use Your Data

All data collected by ExcelCitizen is used solely to:

1. **Track your learning progress** - Show your quiz scores and improvement over time
2. **Personalize your experience** - Remember your language and theme preferences
3. **Enable gamification** - Track achievements, streaks, and XP progression
4. **Improve study efficiency** - Identify areas needing more practice

---

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage Only
All your data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by the iOS operating system. No data is transmitted to external servers.

### Data Persistence
Your data persists on your device until you:
- Uninstall the app
- Clear app data in device settings
- Reset your progress within the app

### No Cloud Sync
Currently, ExcelCitizen does not offer cloud synchronization. Your data exists only on the device where you use the app.

---

## Third-Party Services

ExcelCitizen does **NOT** integrate with:

- ❌ Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
- ❌ Advertising networks
- ❌ Social media platforms
- ❌ Crash reporting services
- ❌ Any third-party data collectors

## Children's Privacy

ExcelCitizen is rated 4+ and is suitable for users of all ages. We do not:

- Collect personal information from children
- Require account creation
- Include social features
- Display advertisements
- Offer in-app purchases to children

Parents can feel confident that their children can use this app safely.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

### Access Your Data
All your data is visible within the app through the Progress and Settings screens.

### Delete Your Data
You can delete your data at any time by:
1. Using the "Reset Progress" option in Settings
2. Uninstalling the app from your device

### Data Portability
Since all data is stored locally on your device, you control your data completely.
